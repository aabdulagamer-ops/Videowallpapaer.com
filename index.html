Detailed Tutorial

Step 1: Create an Account

Click the "Profile" button in the top-right corner to open the account panel. Fill in your email and password, then click "Sign Up" to create your account. You can also sign in with Google for faster access.

Step 2: Download the App

Once signed in, the download button will be unlocked. Click "Download Video Wallpaper for Windows" to download the installer file (VideoWallpaper.exe).

Step 3: Install the App

Run the downloaded .exe file and follow the installation wizard. Choose your preferred installation location and complete the setup.

Step 4: Launch the App

After installation, launch Video Wallpaper from your desktop or start menu. The app will appear in your system tray.

Step 5: Choose Your Video

Right-click the app icon in the system tray and select "Open" or "Settings". Browse and select your desired video file from your computer.

Step 6: Set as Wallpaper

In the app settings, click "Apply" or "Set as Wallpaper". Your desktop background will now display the live video. You can adjust playback settings like volume, loop, and quality.

Tips & Troubleshooting

- Ensure your video file is in a supported format (MP4, AVI, etc.)
- For best performance, use videos under 100MB
- If the wallpaper doesn't apply, try running the app as administrator
- Check Windows settings to allow the app to run in the background
